Services

Initial visit to discuss your requirements - Free of charge

Visits will be carried out between 8AM and 7PM and include:

  • Cleaning bowls and feeding station daily
  • Provide fresh food and water daily
  • Clean/change the litter daily as applicable
  • Deliver/apply medication including tablets & injections
  • Play, cuddle and groom (should your cat wish)
  • Daily update by text, email or WhatsApp (should you wish)
  • Should it be necessary, we will take your cat to your vet (the cost of the vet is at your expense).
Additional services:
  • We can alternate lights, opening/closing curtains, bring in the bins and collect the mail from your mail box.
  • We are happy to look after additional small pets such as hamsters, guinea pigs, fish.
  • Watering your indoor plants. Should you wish us to water your garden, there is an extra charge of £5 per visit.

Prices

£15 for 1 visit daily
£27 for 2 visits daily

Holiday period (Bank holiday weekends: Friday to Monday inclusive; Easter: Friday to Monday inclusive; Christmas and New Year: from 23rd December to 2nd January inclusive):

£20 for 1 visit daily
£35 for 2 visits daily

Bookings

For new clients we require 7 days’ notice to allow for a free initial consultation.
For existing clients we require a minimum of 48 hours’ notice.

Payment

Bank transfer or cash prior to the visit/s.

Keys collection / key return

First collection is free of charge.
We can keep hold of your keys for future bookings.
If you prefer that keys are collected prior to each booking and returned once you are back at home, a £20 charge will apply.

Security

Keys are kept securely and with no identifying labels and we do not use branded vehicles or uniforms.

Parking

Should you live in an area with residents only or very limited pay and display parking, we would be grateful if you could supply parking permits for each visit.
