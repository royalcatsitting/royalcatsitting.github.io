Privacy Policy

COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY

Royal Cat Sitting is comitted to protecting your privacy. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

The notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the website. Royal Cat Sitting is the sole owner of the information collected on this website. The types of personal information collected are:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number

USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We may use your information to notify you about changes to the functionality of our website or to send you offers or information which we think you may be interested in. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. We will never sell, trade or rent information about you to others. If at anytime you decide you no longer want to be contacted by us, please contact us and we will remove you from the Royal Cat Sitting database.

SECURITY

As required by the Data Protection Act 1998, we here at Royal Cat Sitting follow strict security procedures and take appropriate measures to ensure that the information about you is not disclosed to a third party without your permission and to prevent unauthorised access to it. To prevent unauthorised access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

COOKIES

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site.

LEGAL DISCLAIMER

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with the current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our website.

QUESTIONS

Should you have any questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please contact Royal Cat Sitting.
